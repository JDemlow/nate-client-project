Our Values

We believe that how we work is just as important as what we do. Our work is centered around our core values of equity, community, and transparency.

Equity

We believe that honoring individuals' unique needs helps advance social justice...

  • We meet people where they are by tailoring our services and prices to each client’s goals, financial status, and access to other resources.
  • We try to acknowledge and value the diversity of our clients’ organizations in all the work we do.
  • We center voices from marginalized communities in our work to learn how to be more equitable.

Community

We believe that our community helps us succeed, so we should give back to it in return...

  • We cater our services to nonprofits and small businesses that share our emphasis on community.
  • We donate at least 5% of our revenues up to $300K and 25% of our revenues over $300K to grassroots and mutual aid groups who struggle to access traditional funding.
  • We volunteer in our community for at least two hours each week.

Transparency

We believe that equal and open access to information disrupts unfair power dynamics...

  • We give clients a complete upfront cost before they pay us anything.
  • We send our clients quarterly donation reports so they can see how much and which organizations we donate to.
  • We will coordinate translation and interpretation services if you have employees who communicate in languages other than English.
